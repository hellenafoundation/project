About Helena Foundation

Our mission to empower vulnerable communities

Who We Are

Helena Foundation (HF) is a non-governmental, non-profit organisation grounded in the principles of humanism, freedom, equality, and solidarity. Since our founding in June 2021, we have been known for our swift and effective response to crises.

We are a community-based organisation for Refugee women leaders dedicated to saving lives, empowering through education, protecting the rights and justice of children, women, and feminists from gender and age discrimination.

We focus on preventing and alleviating chronic diseases in children and adults with advanced solutions from biomedical research.
